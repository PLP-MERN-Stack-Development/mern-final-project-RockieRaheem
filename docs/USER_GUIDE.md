## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. **Visit EduLink UG**: Go to https://edulink-ug.com
2. **Click "Sign Up"**: Found in the top right corner
3. **Fill in your details**:
   - Full Name
   - Email Address
   - Password (at least 8 characters)
   - School Name
   - District
   - Education Level (O-Level or A-Level)
   - Subjects you study
4. **Select your role**:
   - Student (default)
   - Teacher (requires verification)
5. **Click "Create Account"**

### Logging In

1. Click **"Login"** button
2. Enter your **email** and **password**
3. Click **"Sign In"**

## For Students

### üîç Browsing Questions

**Finding Questions**:

- Use the search bar to find specific topics
- Filter by **Subject** (Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, etc.)
- Filter by **Education Level** (O-Level or A-Level)
- Sort by **Most Recent**, **Most Upvoted**, or **Most Viewed**

**Reading Questions**:

- Click any question to see full details
- View attachments (images, diagrams)
- Read all answers and comments
- See which answers are verified by teachers

### ‚ùì Asking Questions

**How to Ask a Good Question**:

1. Click the **"Ask Question"** button
2. Write a **clear, specific title**
   - ‚úÖ Good: "How do you solve quadratic equations with negative discriminants?"
   - ‚ùå Bad: "Math help needed"
3. Provide **detailed description**:
   - Explain what you've tried
   - Show your work if applicable
   - Mention specific concepts you're struggling with
4. **Select subject and topic**
5. **Add attachments** if needed (photos of problems, diagrams)
6. **Choose anonymous** if you prefer privacy
7. Click **"Post Question"**

**Anonymous Questions**:

- Your identity is hidden from other students
- Teachers and moderators can still see who posted (for safety)
- Use for sensitive topics or if you're shy

### ‚úçÔ∏è Answering Questions

**How to Provide Great Answers**:

1. Click **"Answer"** on any question
2. Write a **clear, step-by-step explanation**
3. Use proper formatting:
   - Break into paragraphs
   - Use numbered steps for processes
   - Add formulas and equations
4. **Attach images** if helpful (diagrams, worked examples)
5. Click **"Submit Answer"**

**Answer Quality Tips**:

- ‚úÖ Explain concepts, don't just give answers
- ‚úÖ Show your working/reasoning
- ‚úÖ Use simple, clear language
- ‚úÖ Be patient and encouraging
- ‚ùå Don't just post final answers without explanation
- ‚ùå Don't copy from other sources without attribution

### üìä Reputation System

**Earning Reputation Points**:

- **+10** points: Your answer gets upvoted
- **+15** points: Your answer is verified by a teacher
- **+25** points: Your answer is accepted by the question author
- **+5** points: Your question gets upvoted
- **-2** points: Your answer/question gets downvoted

**Reputation Benefits**:

- Unlock badges and achievements
- Build credibility in the community
- Get featured as a top contributor
- Access exclusive features (coming soon)

### ü§ñ Using EduBot (AI Assistant)

**How to Chat with EduBot**:

1. Click the **EduBot icon** (chat bubble in corner)
2. Type your question
3. Get instant AI-powered help!

**What EduBot Can Do**:

- Explain concepts in simple terms
- Help with homework problems (step-by-step)
- Provide study tips and strategies
- Suggest resources for topics
- Answer subject-specific questions

**Best Practices**:

- Ask specific questions
- Provide context (your grade, subject)
- Ask follow-up questions for clarification
- Use it as a learning tool, not just for answers

**Example Conversations**:

```
You: Explain photosynthesis for O-Level
EduBot: Photosynthesis is the process plants use to make food...

You: How do I solve for x in 2x + 5 = 15?
EduBot: Let's solve step by step:
1. Subtract 5 from both sides: 2x = 10
2. Divide both sides by 2: x = 5
```

## For Teachers

### üë®‚Äçüè´ Teacher Verification

To become a verified teacher:

1. Register with role "Teacher"
2. Contact admin with:
   - School name
   - Teacher ID or proof of employment
   - Subjects you teach
3. Wait for verification (usually 24-48 hours)

### ‚úÖ Verifying Answers

**Why Verify?**:

- Helps students identify correct, high-quality answers
- Builds trust in the platform
- Rewards students who provide good help

**How to Verify**:

1. Review student answers thoroughly
2. Check for:
   - Correctness
   - Clear explanation
   - Proper methodology
3. Click **"Verify Answer"** if it meets standards
4. Add optional comment for improvement

**Verification Guidelines**:

- Only verify completely correct answers
- Prioritize answers with good explanations
- Don't verify if there are errors, even minor ones
- Provide feedback on unverified answers

### üéì Hosting Study Sessions

**Creating a Session**:

1. Go to **"Study Sessions"**
2. Click **"Host Session"**
3. Fill in details:
   - **Title**: Clear, descriptive name
   - **Subject & Topic**: What you'll cover
   - **Date & Time**: When it starts
   - **Duration**: How long (30-120 minutes)
   - **Max Participants**: Student limit
   - **Description**: What to expect, prerequisites
4. Click **"Schedule Session"**

**Session Best Practices**:

- Prepare materials in advance
- Start and end on time
- Encourage student participation
- Use interactive features (polls, whiteboard)
- Record session (with permission) for later viewing

**During the Session**:

- Greet students as they join
- Present clearly with visual aids
- Pause for questions regularly
- Monitor chat for questions
- Summarize key points at end

## Troubleshooting

### Login Issues

**Problem**: Can't log in
**Solutions**:

- Check email/password spelling
- Try **"Forgot Password"** to reset
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Try different browser
- Contact support if issue persists

### Video/Audio Not Working

**Problem**: Can't hear or be heard in sessions
**Solutions**:

- Check device permissions (camera, microphone)
- Test in browser settings first
- Try refreshing the page
- Check internet connection
- Use headphones to reduce echo

### Slow Loading

**Problem**: Website loads slowly
**Solutions**:

- Check internet connection
- Close other tabs/applications
- Clear browser cache
- Try different browser
- Check if MongoDB is running (developers)

### Notifications Not Appearing

**Problem**: Missing notifications
**Solutions**:

- Enable browser notifications
- Check notification settings in profile
- Verify email settings
- Check spam folder for emails

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: Is EduLink UG free?**  
A: Yes! EduLink UG is completely free for all Ugandan secondary school students.

**Q: What subjects are covered?**  
A: All O-Level and A-Level subjects including Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, English, History, Geography, and more.

**Q: Can I use it on my phone?**  
A: Yes! The website is mobile-friendly and works on any device with a browser.

**Q: Do I need to be online all the time?**  
A: Most features require internet, but we're working on offline support for core features.

### Account Questions

**Q: Can I change my email?**  
A: Yes, go to Profile Settings ‚Üí Account ‚Üí Change Email.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**  
A: Contact support at support@edulink-ug.com with your request.

**Q: Can I have multiple accounts?**  
A: No, one account per person. Multiple accounts may result in ban.

### Content Questions

**Q: Can I delete my questions/answers?**  
A: Yes, you can delete your own content anytime from your profile.

**Q: What if I post wrong information?**  
A: Edit or delete your answer immediately. Repeatedly posting wrong information may affect your reputation.

**Q: Can I ask homework questions?**  
A: Yes, but explain what you've tried first. We encourage learning, not just getting answers.

### Technical Questions

**Q: Which browsers are supported?**  
A: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge (latest versions).

**Q: Why is my video lagging in sessions?**  
A: This may be due to slow internet. Try turning off camera and using audio only.

**Q: How do I report a bug?**  
A: Email bugs@edulink-ug.com with details and screenshots.
